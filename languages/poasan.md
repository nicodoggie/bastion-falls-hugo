# Poasan (WIP)

## Quick and dirty phonology:

consonants:
P: p b t d k ?
F: s sh h
N: n
A: r
L: l 
vowels:
a i o u

syllables:
CV
CVV

/h/ is the only word-initial fricative
/n/ cannot be word-initial
